\section{Mathematical Foundations}

\subsection{Algebra and Analysis}

The quadratic formula is fundamental in algebra:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

For real analysis, we often work with limits such as:

$$\lim_{x \to \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{x}\right)^x = e$$

\subsection{Calculus}

The power rule for derivatives: $\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$

Integration by parts: $\int u \, dv = uv - \int v \, du$

A classic definite integral:

$$\int_0^{\pi} \sin(x) \, dx = 2$$

\subsection{Linear Algebra}

Matrix multiplication: If $A$ is $m \times n$ and $B$ is $n \times p$, then $AB$ is $m \times p$.

The determinant of a 2x2 matrix:

$$\det\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} = ad - bc$$

Identity matrix: $I = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix}$

\section{Advanced Topics}

\subsection{Series and Sequences}

The Taylor series expansion:

$$f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n$$

The geometric series: $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} r^n = \frac{1}{1-r}$ for $|r| < 1$

\subsection{Differential Equations}

A first-order linear ODE: $\frac{dy}{dx} + P(x)y = Q(x)$

The heat equation in one dimension:

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = \alpha \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}$$

\subsection{Probability and Statistics}

The normal distribution probability density function:

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}$$

Expected value: $E[X] = \sum_{i} x_i P(x_i)$ for discrete random variables.

\section{Logic and Set Theory}

\subsection{Set Operations}

Basic set operations: $A \cup B$ (union), $A \cap B$ (intersection), $A \setminus B$ (difference)

Set membership: $x \in A$ (x is an element of A)

Subset relation: $A \subseteq B$ means every element of $A$ is also in $B$

\subsection{Logic Symbols}

Logical operators: $\land$ (and), $\lor$ (or), $\neg$ (not), $\implies$ (implies), $\iff$ (if and only if)

Quantifiers: $\forall$ (for all), $\exists$ (there exists)

Example: $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}, \exists y \in \mathbb{R} : y > x$
